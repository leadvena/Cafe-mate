# CafeMate Product Definition

## Vision
CafeMate is a premium SaaS product for cafe and restaurant owners to digitize their menus. It focuses on speed, simplicity, and high-end aesthetics.

## Users
Cafe and restaurant owners who want a professional digital presence without the complexity of building a full app.

## Key Features
- **Landing Page**: High-conversion, editorial-style marketing page.
- **Auth**: Frictionless Firebase Auth (Email/Google).
- **Dashboard**: Central hub for menu management and QR code access.
- **Menu Editor**: Real-time, tactile editor with drag-to-reorder and auto-save.
- **Public Menu**: Premium, mobile-first menu experience for customers.
- **QR Code Management**: Dynamic QR codes that point to the cafe's slug.

## Tone & Voice
Warm, professional, human, and artisanal. Avoid corporate jargon.

## Register
- **Brand**: Landing Page.
- **Product**: Dashboard, Editor, Public Menu.
